Exercise Guide

Form cues for key lifts — read before you lift
Universal cues — every lift
Breathe
Exhale on exertion (the push or pull), inhale on the return. Never hold your breath on heavy sets.
Tempo
1-2 sec concentric (moving weight), 2-3 sec eccentric (lowering). Slow lowering builds significantly more muscle.
Mind-muscle connection
Feel the target muscle working. If you cannot feel it, lighten the weight and focus on the squeeze.
Neutral spine
On every compound lift maintain natural spine curve. Never round your lower back under any load.
